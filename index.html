Welcome to 2021 which has been declared our year of “TURN AROUND ERA”

Pastor's Desk

Turn Around greetings in the name of Jesus.

With so much joy, I welcome you to the Year 2021 which has been declared by the presiding Bishop over this commission as our “TURN AROUND ERA”, indeed it is your turn for a turnaround encounter. The year has begun and it is the will of God for us to end it gloriously, but how will this be achieved, because anyone that desires harvest first sows a... seed.
The month of January is our seed sowing month, it is our month of “PRAYER AND FASTING EMPOWERS THE FULFILLMENT OF PROPHECY” 1 Tim. 1 :18 We know that every prophetic word concerning our life is meant to be fulfilled because every prophetic agenda is a spiritual open door which usually attracts great opposition. This is the race set before us and with prayer and fasting we can oppose all forces of darkness and end our year gloriously as God has made it. Mat. 17: 19-21.
Push out all distractions and focus on making the best out of this period. Engage in the prayers, read the prophetic books for the month and the heavens shall be open on your behalf.
You are set for a Turnaround Encounter, I love you.
Remain ever blessed.
PASTOR AFOLABI, TESTIMONY
RESIDENT PASTOR
LFC, EKET.
