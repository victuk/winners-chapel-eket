Pastor's Desk

Breaking Limits greetings in the name of Jesus.

Breaking Limits greetings in the name of Jesus. With so much joy, I welcome you to the month of November. Our month of unusual breakthrough via unusual praise. I certainly believe that you have been imparted with the grace to break limits by engaging the master... key which is love. Remember, you must continue in that same grace by winning the lost souls back to Christ, thereby depopulating hell and populating heaven which is the prove of your genuine love for God. The month of November has been declared by the Presiding Bishop over this commission, Bishop David Oyedepo, through the help of the holy spirit as “PRAISE FACILITATES FULFILMENT OF PROPHECIES” Rom 4: 17 – 20. God is committed to the full delivery of the Breaking Limit prophetic package ordained for every winner this year, 2020. He says in his word in Isa 55 :10 – 11, that his sent word to us will not return back to him void but it will accomplish that which it was sent for. This is a full assurance that God will always play his part and it is left for us to play our part which is giving to God what he cannot give himself, “PRAISE”. This season is a prophetic season, do not joke about it. Engage yourself in all services and activities for the month and crown it all by passionately giving to God, quality praise in all you do and see if God will not break protocols on your behalf. Engage in high praises for your high places and for the fulfilment of God’s prophecies in your life.
